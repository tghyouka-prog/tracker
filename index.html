Protocol reference
Progressive overload
Log every session: date · exercise · weight · sets · reps. Without this the program drifts to maintenance within 4–6 weeks regardless of everything else.
Zone 2 cardio — Day 5 off day · Post-Push session
Zone 2 builds VO2 max, improves fat oxidation at rest, reduces resting heart rate, and lowers chronic cortisol — directly relevant to shift work.
  • Duration30 min
  • TimingImmediately post-Push B · Day 5 only
  • ModalityBrisk walk outdoors or easy cycle
  • Target HR120–140 BPM
  • Talk testShould hold a conversation — if not, slow down
  • RPE4–5 / 10 — slightly uncomfortable but fully sustainable
Do not run. Brisk walking keeps you in Zone 2 reliably without adding recovery demand.
Warm-up — 10 min mandatory
With scoliosis, loading a cold spine in rotational asymmetry is the primary injury vector. Non-negotiable before every session.
  • Dead Hang first thing, every session3 × 30–45s
  • Cat-Cow2 × 10 slow
  • World's Greatest Stretch5/side
  • 90/90 Hip Switches extra time on tighter side10 reps
Leg days
  • Banded Clamshells2 × 15/side
Push days
  • Band Pull-Aparts2 × 20
Deload — every 4th microcycle
Deload is mandatory — not optional. Without it, cumulative fatigue masks fitness and injury risk climbs. You deload when the protocol says, not when you feel tired.
  • VolumeDrop to 2 sets per exercise (from 3–4)
  • Intensity60–70% of your recent working weight
  • FrequencySame session split — don't skip days
  • DurationOne full 6-day cycle
  • CardioZone 2 only — no high-intensity
  • NutritionKeep protein at target — this is not a diet phase
  • SleepPrioritise extra sleep during deload week — adaptation happens here
Scoliosis note: deload week is when postural corrections consolidate. Keep the daily dead hang and cat-cow — these are non-negotiable even in deload.

Scoliosis — movement contraindications
  • ×
    Barbell back squat — axial compression through a rotated spine. Hack squat machine is the permanent replacement.
  • ×
    Flat barbell bench press — asymmetric thoracic loading under fatigue. Flat machine press replaces it.
  • ×
    Bilateral barbell RDL — axial spinal load, amplifies hip asymmetry. Single-leg DB RDL replaces it.
  • ×
    Bilateral pullover (DB or machine) — long lever thoracic compression. Single-arm cable pullover replaces it.
  • ×
    Close-grip barbell bench — spinal loading under fatigue unjustified. Machine press or weighted dips replace it.
  • ×
    Ab wheel rollouts / weighted cable crunches — heavy spinal flexion contraindicated. Pallof Press, Side Plank, Dead Bug, Bird Dog replace them.
Shift work — recovery rules
  • ×
    Gym before or after a cover callout shift — session postponed to next available off day without exception.
  • ×
    Bare eyes on post-night shift commute — blue-block glasses on inside the building before the exit door opens.
  • ×
    Caffeine after ~10 PM — still 25–50% active at the 9:30 AM sleep window. Confirmed absent and cleared.
  • ×
    Resistance training on Night Shift 2 — CNS is in the deep trench. Zone 2 walk only.
Nutrition — rejected options
  • ×
    Colostrum — cut. Sub-threshold at 1g. Rokeby Fitmilk and Chobani cover gut integrity passively. Cost not justified at 10g+.
  • ×
    Separate Vitamin D3 — cut. LE Two-Per-Day at full 2-capsule serving delivers 2,000 IU D3 — the correct target for an indoor shift worker. Separate D3 creates redundant overlap.
  • ×
    Brazil nut — cut. LE Two-Per-Day at full serving delivers 200mcg selenium — the full RDA. Brazil nut adds 70–90mcg on top pushing combined intake unnecessarily high.
  • ×
    Vanilla / Greek-style yogurt (Farmers Union, Marigold) — thickeners and sugar spike core temperature, disrupting sleep architecture.
  • ×
    Jalna / Alvas — insufficient protein density. Cannot hit 23g casein without gut-distending volume.
  • ×
    Fries at Soup Spoon — pesto rice is the automatic choice. No decision needed at the counter.
  • ×
    Skipping vegetables at takeout — protocol failure. Fiber underpins absorption of the entire supplement stack.
  • ×
    40,000 Volts electrolytes — redundant. Takeout and Rokeby Fitmilk already cover sodium and potassium.
  • ×
    Nin Jiom Pei Pa Koa pre-sleep — sugar load and sensory stimulant. Throat infections only.
